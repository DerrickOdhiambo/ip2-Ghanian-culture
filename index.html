Ghana

Ghana is a country in West-Africa previously known as the gold coast. The Akan people inhabit present day Ghana and parts of Ivory Coast. Akan culture can also be found in the Americas because of the slave trade in the colonial period.

Ghanian Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture, where children are given their first name as ‘day name’ corresponding to the day of the week they were born. This app will calculate the day you were born and allow you to know your Akan name, irregardless of whether you are Akan or not. Enjoy and be sure to try as many dates as possible.
